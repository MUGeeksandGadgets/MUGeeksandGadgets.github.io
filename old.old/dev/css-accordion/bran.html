About First & The Game

FIRST FRC Team 3492 P.A.R.T.s (Putnam Area Robotics Team) is a highschool robotics team in the Wild and Wonderful State of West Virginia.

Our team strives to promote the FIRST Robotics vision of engaging today's youth in S.T.E.M.

This is a varsity sport for the mind.
