Welcome to the News Page!

Welcome to the official Geeks and Gadgets News page! This is the place to come get your news related to all things Geeks and Gadgets. Currently the news archives and current stories folders are empty, all of the news will either be on this page or in our Facebook feed.

Upcoming Events

Geeks and Gadgets 2016 Spring Expo

The Spring Semester is going to be an exciting time for the geeks as we will be having our first bi-annual club expo. We are very excited for the upcoming event. Although our first expo will not be very big, it will show case our collective skills and expertise. We have a whopping grand total of 8 active members working hard to make this expo a success. We do not have a set date or location as of yet, but we will keep you posted as to when and where the magic is going to take place.

Fund Raising

We are planning to kick of this new semester strong with some hardcore fundraising to help make our expo dream a reality. If you would like contribute to the dream, please feel free to contact us. Some of our fund-raising ideas are as follows:

  • Hot Chocolate Sales
  • 3D Printed Valentine Chocolates
  • T-Shirts
  • Renting Puppies
  • 3D Printed Key Chains
And more!

If you have any comments or suggestions, please let us know by emailing us!
